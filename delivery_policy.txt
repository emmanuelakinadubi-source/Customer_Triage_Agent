DELIVERY POLICY

Version: 1.0

Purpose:
This document defines delivery expectations and logistics procedures.

STANDARD DELIVERY TIMES

Domestic Orders:
3–5 business days

International Orders:
7–14 business days

DELIVERY ISSUES

Examples:

* Delayed shipment
* Lost package
* Tracking number not updating
* Wrong delivery address
* Partial shipment

DELAY HANDLING

If shipment exceeds expected delivery window:

1. Create logistics investigation.
2. Notify customer.
3. Monitor shipment status.

INVESTIGATION THRESHOLDS

Domestic:
More than 7 days late

International:
More than 14 days late

OWNER ASSIGNMENT

Category:
Delivery Issue

Assigned Team:
Logistics Team

ESCALATION RULES

Escalate if:

* Customer reports repeated delivery failures.
* Package value exceeds £2000.
* Shipment confirmed lost by carrier.

CUSTOMER COMMUNICATION GUIDELINES

Acknowledge inconvenience.

Do not guarantee delivery dates.

Do not promise compensation unless approved.
