# Return and Refund Policy
Last updated: 10/06/2026

## 1. Timeframe
Customers have **30 days** from the date of delivery to request a return. 

## 2. Condition of Items
To be eligible for a return, items must be:
- Unused and in the same condition that you received them.
- In the original packaging.
- Accompanied by a receipt or proof of purchase.

## 3. Non-Returnable Items
The following goods cannot be returned:
- Gift cards
- Downloadable software products
- Custom-made or personalized items

## 4. Refund Process
Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email to notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment within **7-10 business days**.

## 5. Shipping Costs
You will be responsible for paying for your own shipping costs for returning your item. Shipping costs are non-refundable.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions on how to return your item to us, contact us at syed.fakhar@informationtechconsultants.co.uk
