ESCALATION POLICY

Version: 1.0

Purpose:
This document defines when support requests must be escalated beyond standard processing.

MANAGER ESCALATION CRITERIA

Escalate immediately if customer:

* Threatens legal action.
* Mentions regulatory complaints.
* Requests executive review.
* Reports serious product safety concerns.
* Reports discrimination or misconduct.
* Repeatedly contacts support without resolution.

VIP CUSTOMER ESCALATION

VIP customers should receive priority handling.

Examples:

* Enterprise customers
* Premium subscribers
* Strategic business partners

ABUSIVE LANGUAGE HANDLING

If message contains:

* Threats
* Harassment
* Excessive profanity
* Personal attacks

Set:

Flagged = True

Generate a professional response.

Do not mirror abusive language.

OWNER ASSIGNMENT

Category:
Other

Assigned Team:
Escalate to Manager

CONFIDENCE GUIDELINES

High:
Issue clearly matches one category.

Medium:
Issue partially matches multiple categories.

Low:
Issue is ambiguous or lacks sufficient information.

CUSTOMER COMMUNICATION GUIDELINES

Remain professional.

Avoid emotional language.

Do not argue with customers.

Provide next steps when possible.
