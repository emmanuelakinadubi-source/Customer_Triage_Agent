ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT POLICY

Version: 1.0

Purpose:
This document governs customer account support and account security issues.

ACCOUNT ISSUES COVERED

* Login failures
* Password reset requests
* Account lockouts
* Billing profile issues
* Subscription management issues
* Email update requests

SECURITY REQUIREMENTS

Support agents must verify customer identity before making account changes.

PASSWORD RESET PROCEDURE

1. Verify account ownership.
2. Send secure reset link.
3. Confirm successful access restoration.

LOCKED ACCOUNT PROCEDURE

Priority Level:
High

Reason:
Customer may be unable to access purchases or services.

OWNER ASSIGNMENT

Category:
Account Problem

Assigned Team:
Billing Team

ESCALATION RULES

Escalate if:

* Potential account compromise detected.
* Multiple failed login attempts reported.
* Fraud indicators present.

CUSTOMER COMMUNICATION GUIDELINES

Do not disclose account information.

Do not reveal security verification procedures.

Maintain customer privacy.
